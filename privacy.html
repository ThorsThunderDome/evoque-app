Privacy Policy for Evoque

Last Updated: October 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Evoque. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our application within the Pi Browser.

2. Information We Collect

When you authenticate with your Pi account, we receive the following information from the Pi platform:

  • Your Pi Username
  • Your unique Pi User ID (UID)

If you register as a creator, we also collect the following information that you provide:

  • Your public creator name
  • Your public bio
  • A URL to your profile image

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain the Evoque service.
  • Display your username and creator profile within the app.
  • Link your account to your creator profile and content.
  • (In the future) Process payments and subscriptions via the Pi SDK.

4. Data Storage

Your creator profile information (name, bio, image URL, tiers, posts) is stored securely in our Firebase Firestore database. Your Pi authentication data is handled by the Pi platform and is not stored permanently on our servers.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law. Your public creator profile is, by design, visible to all other users of the Evoque app.
